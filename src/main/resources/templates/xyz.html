Disease: Acute Liver Failure

Description: Rapid loss of liver function, often in people without pre-existing liver disease.

Symptoms:

  • Jaundice
  • Fatigue
  • Abdominal swelling

Medications:

  • Acetylcysteine
  • Liver protectants
  • Antibiotics

Precautions:

  • Avoid alcohol
  • Regular liver function tests
  • Consult a hepatologist

Diet:

  • High-protein diet
  • Green vegetables
  • Low sodium food

Workout: Rest and mild stretching, no intense exercise.

Disease: Altered Sensorium

Description: A state of confusion, drowsiness, or unconsciousness due to neurological issues.

Symptoms:

  • Confusion
  • Loss of alertness
  • Disorientation

Medications:

  • Depends on cause (e.g., antibiotics, anticonvulsants)
  • IV fluids
  • Oxygen support

Precautions:

  • Monitor vitals
  • Avoid driving or hazardous tasks
  • Seek immediate medical attention

Diet:

  • Nutritious, balanced food
  • Stay hydrated
  • Low sugar and salt

Workout: Rest, avoid overexertion, focus on recovery.

Disease: Anxiety

Description: A mental health condition marked by feelings of worry, fear, or nervousness.

Symptoms:

  • Excessive worry
  • Increased heart rate
  • Trouble sleeping

Medications:

  • SSRIs (e.g., Sertraline)
  • Benzodiazepines (short-term)
  • Beta-blockers

Precautions:

  • Avoid caffeine
  • Practice mindfulness
  • Talk to a counselor

Diet:

  • Omega-3 fatty acids
  • Green tea
  • Whole grains

Workout: Deep breathing, meditation, regular light exercises.
